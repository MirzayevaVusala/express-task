About the course

Sometimes movies are more complicated than four-volume
novels. But you learn to read books in school, but you don't
learn to watch movies. And without preparation, it can be
difficult to enjoy watching a movie.

In this 8-session course we will learn how to be conscious
viewers. We will get acquainted with the stages of film
production and learn how to watch a movie through the eyes
of a screenwriter, director and editor. We will understand
genres and look at the most important films, from Citizen
Kane to The Matrix.
